Objective 10 Final Scene

Final Scene - Humanity

This is my final scene for my project. I'd like you to imagine yourself in a modern art museum in a remote region of the world. There is an open-air exhibit in front of you that opens out into the vast desert. This is the scene you see.

Simply titled Humanity, there are many deep meanings that you can infer from the exhibit. On the left side, everything is clear, as if to represent purity and showcase the beauty of the natural world. Then on the right side, everything is glossy, blurry and cloudy, along with a couple spheres that have the Earth on them. Perhaps this represents humanity's negative effects on the Earth and the environment, and our collective conscience, fuzzy with regret which parallels the atmosphere which we have also clouded with greenhouse gases.

Or maybe I'm just going crazy from lack of sleep. Who knows.

Anyway, in addition, this final scene has all of the previously mentioned objectives:

  • Objective 1: Anti-Aliasing
    • Entire image is anti-aliased.
  • Objective 2: Soft Shadows
    • Soft shadows are visible on the floor.
  • Objective 3: Refraction
    • Refraction is visible in the cubes on the left.
    • Refraction is visible in the left sphere in the sky.
  • Objective 6: Glossy Reflection
    • Glossy reflection is visible in the mirrors on the right building.
    • Glossy reflection is visible in the floor.
  • Objective 7: Glossy Transmission
    • Glossy transmission is visible in the right glass panel in front of the stellated dodecahedron.
    • Glossy transmission is visible in the right sphere in the sky.
  • Objective 8: Texture Mapping
    • Texture mapping is visible in the earth textures of the spheres on the right.
    • Texture mapping is visible in the wood texture of the stellated dodecahedron.
  • Objective 9: Normal Mapping
    • Normal mapping is visible in the golf ball normal map of one of the spheres on the right.
    • Normal mapping is visible in the bumpy normal map of the stellated dodecahedron.
